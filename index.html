Entrepreneurship Cell

About Us

Entrepreneurship Cell of Vinod Gupta School of Management is a student-run body that strives towards promoting the ethos of entrepreneurship. We act as a point of contact with various startups and students and help the batch in getting Winter Internships/Live Projects and LBOC Projects(Part of the academic curriculum). E-Cell not only provides academic opportunities but also organizes various annual events to ignite the entrepreneurial spark among the participants.

We conduct events like Srijan, Vichaar, Lovator Pitch, and Socha Hai where we make sure that participants have a lot of fun along with a great learning experience. E-Cell has also played a keen role in launching the Pink Cab Initiative at Kharagpur which helped in enabling local female cab drivers to provide commercial car services.
